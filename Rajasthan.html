Popular Local Cuisines

Awadhi Chicken Biryani
Popular in Lucknow
The Awadhi variant of Biryani originated from none other than the land of Oudh. It has been very different from the other variants of biriyani. The best thing about Awadhi Biryani is that it is less on spices and the focus is on whole spices.
Tunday Kabab
Popular in Lucknow
Tunday Kebab is a popular street food snack from Lucknow. One makes it by finely mincing chicken meat and mixing it with spices like cloves, cinnamon, ginger, and garlic. You can then shape the mixture into kebabs and grill or shallow-fry it until cooked. Tunday Kebabs are known for their tender texture and rich flavours.
Gulab Jamun
Popular in Mathura & Agra
Gulab jamun is a sweet confectionary or dessert, originating in the Indian subcontinent and a type of mithai popular in India, Pakistan, Nepal, the Maldives, and Bangladesh, as well as Myanmar.
Chicken Nihari
Popular in Lucknow
Nihari is a very popular dish in Uttar Pradesh. It is a staple for people who love good food; however, it is primarily available in winter. Professional chefs cook Nihari and serve it with hot kulchas during this time.
