Popular Local Cuisines

Kebabs
Popular in Khan Market
Khan Market is home to several renowned restaurants serving delicious kebabs, including seekh kebabs, galouti kebabs, and shami kebabs. These succulent grilled meat skewers are bursting with flavor and are a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
Street Food in Old Delhi
Popular in Old Delhi
Old Delhi, particularly around Jama Masjid and Chandni Chowk, is a paradise for street food lovers. From spicy chaat (street snacks) like golgappa (pani puri) and papdi chaat to mouthwatering kebabs and samosas, the streets of Old Delhi offer a culinary adventure like no other.
Rabri Faluda
Popular in Karol Bagh
Karol Bagh is renowned for its sweet delicacies, and one must-try dessert is rabri faluda. This indulgent treat consists of layers of creamy rabri (thickened milk) served with faluda (vermicelli noodles) and topped with dry fruits and rose syrup.
Chole Bhature
Popular in Paharganj
Paharganj is known for its delectable chole bhature, a combination of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). You can find numerous street vendors and eateries serving this popular dish.
